Our Foundation

Built on Curiosity.
Driven by Markets.

Founded in April 2024 at Allenhouse Business School, FinBuzz exists to transform finance students into finance professionals — one event, one insight, one connection at a time.

Financial Education
From financial literacy masterclasses to advanced tax and investment workshops — we make finance accessible and actionable for every student.
Competitions & Pitches
Annual stock pitch competitions, case challenges, and AI quiz events that sharpen analytical thinking and competitive edge.
Industry Networking
Partnerships with ICICI Bank, Goldman Sachs alumni, HDFC, and local MSME experts — real connections that open real doors.
Career Readiness
Pre-placement catalyst sessions, MoU-backed internship placements, and mock interviews with industry professionals.

What We Do

Five Pillars of
Financial Mastery

Every FinBuzz activity is designed to move you from textbook knowledge to market-ready confidence.

Stock Pitch Competitions
Members research, analyse, and pitch real equities to expert judges. Develop analyst-grade thinking, build a pitch deck portfolio, and compete for internship leads and certificates.
Annual Event
Financial Literacy Masterclasses
From savings and budgeting fundamentals to advanced tax planning and risk management — interactive workshops led by faculty experts and industry guests.
Workshop Series
Digital Finance & Fintech
Hands-on exploration of UPI ecosystems, crypto fundamentals, and emerging fintech platforms. Group simulations bridge classroom theory with real-world digital banking.
Tech-Finance
Guest Lectures & Expert Talks
Industry professionals from ICICI Bank, Goldman Sachs alumni, and MSME offices bring portfolio management, market strategy, and career navigation to the classroom.
Industry Connect
Pre-Placement Catalyst
A structured career series covering resume building, mock interviews, and finance role preparation. Panels include alumni placed at Goldman Sachs, HDFC, and leading NBFCs.
Career Series
MoU Partnerships & Internships
Active MoU with a local finance firm providing real internship spots. Speed-networking mixers, pitch events, and placement drives — where connections become careers.

Event Chronicle

Glimpses of
FinBuzz in Action

From our founding masterclass to Budget Pe Charcha — every event leaves a mark.

Apr 30, 2024
Inaugural FinBuzz Masterclass: Financial Literacy Series
~40 Attendees ABS Campus

The founding event of FinBuzz. An interactive session on savings, budgeting, and decision-making basics featuring faculty-led demos and open Q&A rounds.

🚀 Highlight: Marked club founding with 200+ social media engagements on launch day.
Aug 23, 2024
Digital Finance Workshop
35 Participants Guest: Bank Professional

Hands-on exploration of fintech tools, UPI ecosystems, and crypto basics. A local bank guest speaker led group simulations bridging theory with real-world digital finance.

💡 Focus: Bridging classroom theory with live fintech applications.
Nov 12, 2024
Guest Lecture: Investment Strategies
50+ Students ICICI Bank Expert

Portfolio management and market trend analysis delivered by an ICICI Bank professional. Interactive polls and live case studies made this a standout session.

🤝 Outcome: 10+ follow-up networking requests from students to speaker.
Apr 15, 2025
Stock Pitch Competition
25 Entrants Exuberance Fest

Annual pitch event where members presented equity analyses to industry judges. Winners received certificates and internship leads. A showcase of real analytical skill.

🏆 Winners received certificates + direct internship referrals.
Oct 12, 2025
Pre-Placement Catalyst, Chapter 1
~45 Attendees Goldman Sachs · HDFC Alumni

Career prep series kickoff: Resume building, finance interview tips, and a panel with alumni placed at Goldman Sachs and HDFC. Live mock interviews with detailed feedback.

🎯 Mock interviews with real-time feedback from industry panellists.

FinBuzz Masterclass: Advanced Literacy

Budget Pe Charcha — FinBuzz Edition
